Technical Architecture

INSILICON-1 packs custom Transformer Processing Units on a 4 nm node, delivering native FP4/INT4 throughput. Compared with NVIDIA H100:

  • Power TDP 150 W vs 700 W
  • Cost $15–20 K vs $30–40 K
